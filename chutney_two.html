GROUNDNUT CHUTNEY

Groundnut chutnery

Groundnut Chutney is a tasty Indian chutney made from roasted groundnuts (peanuts).
The groundnuts are ground with garlic, green chilli, and salt.

Sometimes tamarind or lemon juice is added for a tangy flavor.

It has a creamy texture and a nutty taste.
Groundnut chutney is commonly served with dosa, idli, or snacks.

It adds rich flavor and nutrition to meals.
It can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days.
