Khada Masala

Khada Masala is a mix of whole spices used in Indian cooking.
It includes spices like cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, black pepper, and bay leaves.

These spices are used in whole form instead of being ground.
Khada masala adds strong aroma and rich flavor to dishes.

It is commonly used in biryani, pulao, and curries.

The spices release flavor slowly during cooking.
It makes the food more fragrant and tasty.
