Gruhiniz is a platform designed to support home-based women entrepreneurs by helping them sell handmade and homemade products online. The project focuses on understanding the needs and challenges of both sellers and buyers through empathy mapping. It identifies problems such as limited customer reach, delivery issues, low technical confidence, and trust concerns in online shopping. The platform aims to provide solutions like easy product management, secure payments, customer reviews, and low commission charges. Overall, Gruhiniz helps small businesses grow digitally while providing customers with trusted homemade products.
