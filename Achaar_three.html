Carrot Achaar

Carrot Pickle is a tasty Indian pickle made from fresh carrots and spices.
Carrot pieces are mixed with salt, chilli powder, mustard seeds, and oil.

Sometimes lemon juice or vinegar is added for extra flavor.

The mixture is kept for a few days to absorb the spices.
Carrot achar is commonly served with roti, paratha, or rice.
Carrot achar has a spicy, tangy, and slightly sweet taste.

It adds extra flavor and taste to everyday meals.
Carrot pickle can be stored in airtight containers for several weeks.
