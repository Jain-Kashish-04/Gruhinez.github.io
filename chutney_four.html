Vada Pav Chutney is a spicy chutney used in the popular Mumbai street food vada pav.

The ingredients are ground together to form a dry or slightly coarse chutney.
Salt and a little oil are added to enhance the flavor.

This chutney is spread inside the pav along with the potato vada.
