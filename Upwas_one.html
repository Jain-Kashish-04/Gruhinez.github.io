SHABUDANA PAPAD

Shabudana papad

Sabudana Papdi is a crispy snack made from sabudana (tapioca pearls).
Sabudana is soaked and mixed with spices to make a paste.

These papdi are dried in the sun for a few days.
Before eating, they are deep fried to become crispy.

Garlic chutney is commonly served with snacks, roti, or meals.

Sabudana papdi is light and crunchy in taste.
It is often eaten as a snack or during fasting days.
