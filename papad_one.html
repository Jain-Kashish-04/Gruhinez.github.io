Moong Dal Papad

Moong Dal Papad is a thin and crispy Indian snack made from moong dal (green gram).

It is prepared by grinding soaked moong dal with spices and making a dough.
The dough is rolled into thin round discs and dried in the sun.

Papad is usually roasted or fried before eating.
It is commonly served as a crunchy side dish with Indian meals.
