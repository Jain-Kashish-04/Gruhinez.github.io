Urad Papad

Urad Dal Papad is a thin and crispy Indian snack made from urad dal (black gram) flour.
It is prepared by mixing urad dal flour with salt and spices to make a dough.

The dough is rolled into thin round discs.

These discs are dried in sunlight to preserve them.
Urad papad is usually roasted or fried and served with meals as a crunchy side dish.
