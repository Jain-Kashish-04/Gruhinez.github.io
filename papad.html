MOONG DAL PAPAD

Moong Dal Papad is a thin and crispy Indian snack made from moong dal (green gram).
Papad is usually roasted or fried before eating.
It is commonly served as a crunchy side dish with Indian meals.

RICE PAPAD

Rice Papad is a thin and crispy snack made mainly from rice flour.
It is prepared by mixing rice flour with water, salt, and spices to make a dough.
It is commonly served as a crunchy side dish with Indian meals.





PUNJABI PAPAD

Punjabi Papad is a spicy and crispy Indian papad popular in Punjab.
It is mainly made from urad dal flour mixed with spices.
The dough is rolled into thin discs and dried in sunlight.

URAD PAPAD

Urad Dal Papad is a thin and crispy Indian snack made from urad dal (black gram) flour.

It is prepared by mixing urad dal flour with salt and spices to make a dough.
