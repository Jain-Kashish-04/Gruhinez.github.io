Lemon Achaar

Lemon Pickle is a popular Indian pickle made from fresh lemons and spices.
It is prepared by mixing lemon pieces with salt, chilli powder, and oil.

The mixture is kept in sunlight for several days to develop flavor.

Lemon achar has a sour, spicy, and tangy taste.
It is commonly served with rice, roti, or other Indian meals.
