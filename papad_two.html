Rice papad

Rice Papad is a thin and crispy snack made mainly from rice flour.
It is prepared by mixing rice flour with water, salt, and spices to make a dough.

The dough is shaped into small round discs and dried in the sun.

Before eating, the papad is usually fried or roasted.
It is commonly served as a crunchy side dish with meals or as a snack..
