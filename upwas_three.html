Garlic Chutney is a spicy and flavorful Indian chutney made from fresh garlic cloves.
Garlic is ground with red chilli powder, salt, and spices.

Sometimes oil, lemon juice, or tamarind is added for extra taste.
It has a strong, spicy, and slightly tangy flavor.

Garlic chutney is commonly served with snacks, roti, or meals.

It enhances the taste of food and adds a spicy kick.
It can be stored in an airtight container for several days.
