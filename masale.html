TEA MASALA

Tea Masala is a flavorful spice mixture used to make masala tea.

It is made by grinding spices like cardamom, ginger, cloves, and cinnamon.

GARAM MASALA

Garam Masala is a popular Indian spice blend used in many dishes.

It is made by grinding spices like cumin, coriander, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon.





KALA MASALA

Kala Masala is a traditional Indian spice blend mainly used in Maharashtrian cuisine.

It is made from roasted spices like coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, and dried coconut.

KHADA MASALA

Khada Masala is a mix of whole spices used in Indian cooking.
