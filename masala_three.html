Kala Masala

Kala Masala is a traditional Indian spice blend mainly used in Maharashtrian cuisine.
It is made from roasted spices like coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, and dried coconut.

The spices are roasted and ground into a dark-colored powder.

Kala masala gives a strong aroma and rich flavor to dishes.
It is commonly used in curries, vegetables, and lentil dishes.

This masala adds a slightly smoky and spicy taste to food.
It is an important spice mix in many Maharashtrian recipes.
