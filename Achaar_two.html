Mango Achaar

Mango Pickle is a popular Indian pickle made from raw mangoes and spices.
Raw mango pieces are mixed with salt, chilli powder, and mustard oil.

Various spices like mustard seeds, fenugreek, and turmeric are added for flavor.

The mixture is kept in sunlight for a few days to develop taste.
Mango achar is commonly eaten with rice, roti, and other Indian meals.
