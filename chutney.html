GARLIC CHUTNEY

Garlic Chutney is a spicy and flavorful Indian chutney made from fresh garlic cloves.
Garlic is ground with red chilli powder, salt, and spices.

GROUNDNUT CHUTNEY

Groundnut Chutney is a tasty Indian chutney made from roasted groundnuts (peanuts).
The groundnuts are ground with garlic, green chilli, and salt.





COCONUT CHUTNEY

Coconut Chutney is a popular South Indian chutney made from fresh coconut.
Grated coconut is blended with green chilli, ginger, and salt.

Sometimes roasted chana dal or peanuts are added for thickness.

VADAPAV CHUTNEY

Vada Pav Chutney is a spicy chutney used in the popular Mumbai street food vada pav.

It is mainly made from garlic, dry coconut, peanuts, and red chilli powder.
