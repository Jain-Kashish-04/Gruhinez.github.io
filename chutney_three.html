Coconut Chutney

Coconut Chutney is a popular South Indian chutney made from fresh coconut.
Grated coconut is blended with green chilli, ginger, and salt.

Tamarind or lemon juice can be added for a slightly tangy taste.
It is usually topped with a tempering of mustard seeds and curry leaves.

Coconut chutney is commonly served with dosa, idli, and vada.

It has a fresh, mild, and creamy flavor that enhances meals.
